# 🚖 Smart Rapido Taxi Demand Predictor

Predict future taxi demand using an **LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) Deep Learning Model**. This application forecasts taxi demand for upcoming hours and provides business recommendations to help taxi operators optimize driver allocation and improve service efficiency.

---

# 📌 Problem Statement

Taxi demand changes continuously throughout the day due to office timings, weather conditions, holidays, weekends, traffic congestion, and public events. Without accurate demand forecasting, taxi service providers often face several operational challenges:

- Drivers remain idle during low-demand periods.
- Customers experience longer waiting times during peak hours.
- Poor driver allocation reduces business efficiency.
- Higher operational costs due to incorrect resource planning.
- Difficulty in planning future taxi availability.

To solve these challenges, an intelligent forecasting system is required that can predict future taxi demand using historical demand data.

---

# 💡 Solution

The **Smart Rapido Taxi Demand Predictor** uses a trained **LSTM Deep Learning model** to analyze historical taxi demand patterns and forecast future demand for the next few hours.

The application provides:

- Future taxi demand prediction
- Hour-wise forecast table
- Interactive demand forecast graph
- Prediction summary
- Business recommendations based on predicted demand

This enables taxi operators to make data-driven decisions, improve fleet utilization, reduce waiting times, and enhance customer satisfaction.
